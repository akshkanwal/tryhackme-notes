## Why TryHackMe

My professional background is in infrastructure, networking, and systems administration — the defensive side of IT. TryHackMe gives me structured exposure to the offensive security perspective: understanding how attacks work, what attackers look for, and how to think about systems the way a threat actor does.

This directly improves my defensive work — configuring firewalls, hardening endpoints, and advising clients on security posture all benefit from understanding what the controls are actually protecting against.
